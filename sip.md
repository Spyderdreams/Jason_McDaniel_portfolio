# SIP Project

## AI-Assisted Microsoft Enterprise Cleanup

**One-liner:** Audit-first PowerShell cleanup for **AD DS**, **Entra ID**, and **Exchange Online**, with business-friendly reporting, policy-aware AI summaries, and approval-gated remediation.

## The problem
Microsoft environments naturally accumulate **stale users, orphaned mailboxes, unused groups, and over-granted permissions**. That “directory debt” increases attack surface, drives licensing waste, and creates cleanup work that teams avoid.

## What makes it innovative
- **Audit-first by default** (no changes) so stakeholders can trust the output.
- **Plain-English reporting** (what was found, why it matters, impact) instead of raw dumps.
- **Approval gate** before any remediation actions execute.
- **Logs + rollback mindset** for safe demos and future production use.
- **Cybersecurity SME review** to validate permissions and remediation safety.
