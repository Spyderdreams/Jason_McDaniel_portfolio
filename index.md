## Current SIP

**AI-Assisted Microsoft Enterprise Cleanup**

An audit-first PowerShell module that reduces identity sprawl across **AD DS**, **Microsoft Entra ID**, and **Exchange Online**.

What it does:
- Runs in **Audit Mode** first (no changes)
- Produces **HTML/CSV** reports that show *what, why, and impact*
- Uses an **approval gate** before any remediation actions execute
- Captures **logs** with a rollback-ready mindset

## What I’m building this semester

A repeatable workflow that goes **audit → explain → approve → remediate**, with safety guardrails and cybersecurity SME review.
